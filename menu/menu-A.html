Assult Rifle AR QBZ-95



QBZ-95 is a bullpup assault rifle designed and manufactured by Norinco, and issued since 1995 as the service rifle for the People's Liberation Army, People's Armed Police and various paramilitary law enforcement agencies in the People's Republic of China. The rifle's designation "QBZ" stands for 'light weapon' in keeping with the coding standards of the Chinese defense industry.
